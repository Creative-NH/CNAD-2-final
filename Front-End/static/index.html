About Us

Why Assess Your Risk?

Falls are one of the leading causes of injury among the elderly, often due to factors like muscle weakness, deteriorating vision, and poor balance.Early detection and intervention can help reduce risks and promote independence.

How it work?

Take the Test – Answer simple questions and perform interactive tasks.

Get Your Results – Instantly see your risk level and insights.

Follow Recommendations – Learn exercises, precautions, and next steps.
